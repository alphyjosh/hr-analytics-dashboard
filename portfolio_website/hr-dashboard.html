About this Dashboard

This interactive HR Analytics Dashboard allows HR professionals to track and analyze staffing levels, attrition rates, and manpower planning metrics. Unlike static reports, users can input their own organization's staffing data and immediately see visualizations and insights based on their real-world numbers.

The dashboard is specifically designed to help organizations with:

  • Monthly tracking of headcount, new joiners, and leavers
  • Calculating and visualizing attrition rates with warning indicators
  • Forecasting future staffing needs based on historical trends
  • Identifying patterns in staffing fluctuations to inform hiring strategies

Key Features

Customizable Data

Input your organization's specific staffing numbers including headcount, new joiners, and leavers for each month.

Visual Analytics

Interactive charts showing headcount trends, staff movement, and attrition rates over time.

Staffing Forecast

Predictive analysis showing projected headcount and hiring needs for future months.

Branding Options

Customize the dashboard with your company name, colors, and title for a personalized experience.

Attrition Alerts

Automatic highlighting of months with high attrition rates that require attention.

Data Export

Export your staffing data to CSV format for further analysis or reporting.
